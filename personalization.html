• About N = 1 Approach

    Iken Personics uses an N=1 approach where every customer is analyzed individually in real time.

    Our powerful personalization engine analyzes each user individually and derives deep context from user‘s actions, taking into account user‘s demography, location, transactions, likes, dislikes and time spent on the content. We provide our clients with one customer view (N=1) which helps in increasing uptake and driving higher revenues. We know what your customers want before they realize it.

    About Bottom Up Approach

    We start by getting to know your customers, dig deep and monitor their dynamic behavior.

    Our solution adopts a bottom-up approach where the behavior of one customer is analyzed at a time and individual learning is summarized to get group, application, functional or organizational level insights. We identify behavioral outliers, patterns, and associated contexts. Every recommendation is prompt by an accurate reflection of the customer‘s current behaviors and needs.

    Next Best Action

    Our proactive personalization platform uses hybrid AI technology.

    We use hybrid AI along with self-learning algorithms to create a proactive personalization platform that holistically understands users‘ behavior. The self-learning algorithm improves with the number of transactions that take place. This, when combined with various filtering techniques derives the wisdom of the crowd and builds association with the content to provide highly targeted recommendations.

     

    We provide your customers with the right offer at the right time.

    Our platform determines Next Best Action for each of your customers at any point of time driven by life - event patterns, buying behavior, social media interactions, and other aspects. Our decision logic combines company‘s business rules with machine learning algorithms to enable the company to respond to the customer‘s needs during the interaction, while ensuring that the action taken also benefits the company.
