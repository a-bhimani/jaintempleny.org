• Member Benefits

    Be proud to be a member of JTNY and help strengthen our Jain community.

    • Voting rights in temple governance (effective from the year following membership)
    • Eligibility to join Executive Committee and Board of Trustees
    • Access to all temple events and celebrations
    • Priority registration for Pathshala and youth programs
    • Regular updates on temple activities and news

    Membership Types

    • Life Membership: $201 (Single/Family)
    • Patron Membership: $25,000 (Single/Family)
    • Benefactor Membership: $100,000 (Single/Family)

    How to Join

    Complete the membership form online. You can pay by check payable to "Jain Temple of New York" or submit payment at the temple.

    Upgrades & Pledges

    Existing members can upgrade their membership level or make pledges using the same form.
