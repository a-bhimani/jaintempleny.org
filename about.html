• In today‘s world, Smartphones have taken centre-stage, and all web based consumer businesses are shifting towards mobile platforms, building Apps to connect with their consumers better. With the limited form factor, time and space being relative, the in-depth analysis of customer is becoming the need of the hour for the mobile landscape.

    Iken Personics fulfils that need by providing the consumer facing businesses with a revolutionary personalization platform, Mooga. Our customer analytics platform can catalyse the growth of your business by providing deep personalization through hybrid AI (Artificial intelligence) for the mobile ecosystem.

    Iken Personics is a SINE, IIT-Bombay incubated startup that specializes in personalization to enable superior end - user experience. Mooga creates a single customer view, enabling intelligent recommendations driven by its self - learning based automated tool. It can gain insight into the customer base at the granularity level of N = 1 and be the game changer for all businesses.
