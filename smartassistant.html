Types of Campaigns

New Leads

Engaging leads as soon as they are acquired in real-time to keep the leads warm for higher conversion rate

Customer Onboarding

Automating the entire process that users go through when they start their journey as a customer of your product or service. Like Signup , Documentation, FAQ’s , Education etc

Aged Leads

Leads That have not been engaged for more than a week through any channel. Engage with Stale leads with reminders of their Requests.

Uncontactable Leads

Leads that were not contactable through various channels or after several attempts Texting using Chat Interface can re-engage dead Leads

Incomplete Applications

Prompt the users who are yet to finish the applications.to complete the application through Chat texts

Event Follow-ups

Follow up with the Leads who have RSVP'd but have not Shown up. Follow up with the Leads who have RSVP'd but have not
