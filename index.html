• अहिंसा | Ahimsa — Compassion for all living beings

    सत्य | Satya — Speak truth with kindness

    अस्तेय | Asteya — Take nothing that is not given

    ब्रह्मचर्य | Brahmacharya — Purity of mind and conduct

    अपरिग्रह | Aparigraha — Detach from material desires

  • णमो अरिहंताणं | Namo Arihantanam — I bow to the Arihantas

    णमो सिद्धाणं | Namo Siddhanam — I bow to the Siddhas

    णमो आयरियाणं | Namo Ayariyanam — I bow to the Acharyas

    णमो उवज्झायाणं | Namo Uvajjhayanam — I bow to the Upadhyayas

    णमो लोए सव्व साहूणं | Namo Loe Savva Sahunam — I bow to all the Sadhus
