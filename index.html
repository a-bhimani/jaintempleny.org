• अहिंसा Ahimsa — Compassion for all living beings

    सत्य Satya — Speak truth with kindness

    अस्तेय Asteya — Take nothing that is not given

    ब्रह्मचर्य Brahmacharya — Purity of mind and conduct

    अपरिग्रह Aparigraha — Detach from material desires

  • णमो अरिहंताणं — I bow to the Arihantas

    णमो सिद्धाणं — I bow to the Siddhas

    णमो आयरियाणं — I bow to the Acharyas

    णमो उवज्झायाणं — I bow to the Upadhyayas

    णमो लोए सव्व साहूणं — I bow to all the Sadhus
